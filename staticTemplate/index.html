Tic Tac Toe Rules && Guide
How To Play?

1. Select the number of players (up to 4 players). Choose how many players will participate in the game.

2. Once you've selected your players, click "Start Game" to begin the game and play with your co-players.

3. After that, you'll be taken to a different UI where the game will begin.

Ingame Rules

1. When the game starts, you can select players and click "Start Game" to initiate the game. You can also add more players if needed.

2. If necessary, you can reset the game by clicking the "Reset" button.

3. You can return to the homepage by clicking the "Home" icon.

Win or Draw:
  • The game has two possible outcomes: Win or Draw.
  • If the game ends, and either a player wins or the game ends in a draw, click the "OK" button to start a new game.
  • In 2-player and 3-player modes, the game requires a line of three tables to determine the winner.
  • In 4-player mode, a line of four tables is required to determine the winner.
Reminder:

Player1 will always take the first turn in the game.

Players take turns starting with Player 1, rotating through the selected number of players.
